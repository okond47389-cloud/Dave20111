Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optional filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximatley 10 minutes
  • Preparaton: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 table spoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling: cheese, diced vegetable, cooked meat, herbs

Insturctions

  1. Beat the eggs: in a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. you can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a huffier texture
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is mealted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add filling (optional): When the eggs begins to set at the edges but slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your choice of fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette coutinues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it and over the filling. Let it cook for another minutes, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed
